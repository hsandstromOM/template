Vernita Brown

Manager, Culture, Communications, & Operations

Originally from Tampa, Florida, Vernita fell in love with South Carolina on a visit to Clemson University, where she earned both her Bachelor and Masters degrees. After graduation, she went on to do youth development work with a community benefit organization in the Bahamas and subsequently returned to the States to work with various non-profits. This non-profit work allowed Vernita the opportunity to provide support to non-traditional, impoverished college students as well as the opportunity to coach other community programs towards quality standards. Vernita is thrilled to be on the TFA-SC team, where she can be a part of a great movement towards ONE DAY.
